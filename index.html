Responsible Data Use Playbooks

The Responsible Data Use Playbooks provide actionable steps to public and private sector leaders who want to use and share data to increase the impact of their COVID-19 response and plan for the future. The playbooks give leaders of these organizations both a framework for designing data collaborations, as well as specific steps they can begin implementing immediately.

Responsible Data Use Playbook for Digital Contact Tracing: Equips public health authorities and application developers with information and protocols for preserving privacy and ensuring the ethical use of data within contact tracing efforts.

Responsible Data Use Playbook for Job Seeker Tools: Provides actionable steps that government agencies and private organizations can take to collaboratively use data to provide recently laid-off workers with increased education and career opportunities, as well as skills training and social support services.
